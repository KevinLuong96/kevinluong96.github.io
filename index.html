Hey! Thanks for visiting my website.

I am a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo that's set to graduate in 2020. I love creating things, whether it be with my own two hands, with a couple strokes on the keyboard, or even with scissors and some glue. The first time I 3D printed a housing for my first year design project that I created on SolidWorks, I remember holding it in my hands and thinking "Wow! I made it and its not horrible!" Since then, I've learned more about the world of software and it feels like the only thing I've learned is how much more there is to learn! On this website you'll find a collection of past, present and future projects, I'll update it regularly as I make more things so stay tuned!
